This module is the culminating presentation sequence for the course. Students bring together technical explanation, ethical analysis, stakeholder reasoning, and governance recommendations into a public-facing form that helps non-specialists understand both how an AI system works and why it matters.

The final work should show how a concrete AI system operates and why that operation has public stakes. Students are expected to make mechanisms understandable without flattening their complexity, and to connect risks or benefits to practical questions of oversight, accountability, and public value.
