Theories that explain why consistent patterns of AI use and deployment keep happening.

These cards provide the theoretical foundations that explain why the deployment patterns occur. Where the deployment pattern cards ask *what do you notice*, these cards ask *why does that keep happening*. Each one introduces a concept from science and technology studies — a field that examines how technical systems and social worlds shape each other. These are not ethical judgments about whether something is acceptable. They are descriptive tools for understanding the structural forces behind what you observe. Use them when you want to move from noticing a pattern to explaining it — in an analysis, a paper, or a conversation about why things work the way they do.
